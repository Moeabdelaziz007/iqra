# The Pattern of Mercy (نمط الرحمة)

## Quranic References
- **Surah Al-A'raf (7:156):** "...My mercy encompasses all things." (وَرَحْمَتِي وَسِعَتْ كُلَّ شَيْءٍ)
- **Surah Al-An'am (6:12):** "He has prescribed for Himself mercy." (كَتَبَ عَلَىٰ نَفْسِهِ الرَّحْمَةَ)
- **Surah Al-Anbiya (21:107):** "And We have not sent you, [O Muhammad], except as a mercy to the worlds." (وَمَا أَرْسَلْنَاكَ إِلَّا رَحْمَةً لِّلْعَالَمِينَ)

## Tafsir Summary (Ibn Kathir)
Ibn Kathir emphasizes that Allah's mercy is His primary attribute in dealing with His creation. It precedes His wrath. The sending of prophets, the revelation of scriptures, and the provision of sustenance are all manifestations of this divine mercy. Even in the stories of destroyed nations, mercy is shown to the believers who are saved.

## Patterns of Mercy
- **The 99:1 Ratio:** Based on the Hadith, Allah kept 99 parts of mercy for the Hereafter and sent down 1 part for all creation.
- **Precedence over Wrath:** The divine decree that mercy overcomes anger.
- **Universal Reach:** Mercy is not limited to believers but extends to all living beings in this world.
