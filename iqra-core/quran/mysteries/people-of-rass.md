# The People of Al-Rass (أصحاب الرس)

## Quranic References
- **Surah Al-Furqan (25:38):** "And [We destroyed] 'Aad and Thamud and the companions of the well (Al-Rass)..." (وَعَادًا وَثَمُودَ وَأَصْحَابَ الرَّسِّ)
- **Surah Qaf (50:12):** "The people of Noah denied before them, and the companions of the well (Al-Rass) and Thamud." (كَذَّبَتْ قَبْلَهُمْ قَوْمُ نُوحٍ وَأَصْحَابُ الرَّسِّ وَثَمُودُ)

## Tafsir Summary (Ibn Kathir)
Ibn Kathir mentions several opinions regarding the identity of the People of Al-Rass. Some scholars suggest they were a people who killed their prophet by throwing him into a well (Rass). Others identify them with the people of Shu'ayb or a tribe in Yamamah. The Quran mentions them alongside Aad and Thamud as examples of nations that rejected their messengers and were subsequently destroyed.

## Archaeological/Historical Evidence
- **Linguistic Evidence:** The word "Rass" in Arabic refers to an old well.
- **Geographic Speculation:** Some historians point to the region of Ar-Rass in modern-day Saudi Arabia, though definitive archaeological links to the Quranic people are scarce.

## Open Mysteries
- The specific identity of the prophet sent to them is not explicitly named in the Quran.
- Their exact geographic location and the nature of their civilization remain largely unknown.

## Confidence Levels
- **Quranic Narrative:** 100% (Divine Revelation)
- **Identity of Prophet:** 30% (Based on varied scholarly opinions)
- **Geographic Location:** 20% (Highly speculative)

⚠️ *Note: This civilization remains one of the most enigmatic mentioned in the Quran.*
